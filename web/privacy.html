Overview

SmartPocketBuddy is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Data stored on your device

All of the following stay on your device and are never sent to our servers:

  • Emergency contact — The name and phone number you choose
  • Location — Used only to share with your contact during Safety Mode
  • Recordings — Audio you choose to record, saved locally
  • Event history — Timestamps, locations, and notes from past events

Data sharing

During Safety Mode, your location is shared only with the emergency contact you have configured. This is sent via SMS with a map link. We do not receive, store, or transmit this data.

Third-party services

SmartPocketBuddy uses your device's built-in capabilities (GPS, contacts, microphone) and your phone's SMS to send messages. We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.
